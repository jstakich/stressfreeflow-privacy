Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Overview

Stress Free Flow is a relaxation-focused application designed to help users reduce stress through calming visuals, interactive elements, and soothing ambient sounds.

No Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, track, or share any personal data or user information. You can use all features of the app without creating an account or providing any personal details.

Stress Free Flow does not use analytics, tracking tools, or third-party services that collect user data. Your experience is completely private and focused solely on your well-being.

All features of Stress Free Flow are self-contained within the app. No user data is transmitted to external servers at any time.

App Features & Functionality

The app includes a feature that allows users to share videos with a QR code to share the app with friends. This code links directly to the App Store and does not facilitate external payments.

The Gentle Reminder function reminds the user to use the relaxation app but will only execute if the app is closed. This feature uses standard device functionality, such as local notifications, and does not transmit or store any personal information.

Some features, such as ambient audio, may continue to play while the device is locked. These are user-initiated functions and do not involve any background data collection or tracking.

Background Audio

Stress Free Flow can also be used as a background sound app. Ambient audio may continue playing while the device is locked, which can be helpful for relaxation, rest, sleep support, or focused work.

This feature is optional and must be turned on by the user within the app. To enable it, open Settings, select Audio Options / Vibration, and turn on “Play Sound When Locked.

Wellness Disclaimer

Stress Free Flow is a wellness app designed to provide calming visuals and ambient sounds for relaxation and focus. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
