Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Overview

Stress Free Flow is a relaxation-focused application designed to help users reduce stress through calming visuals, interactive elements, and soothing ambient sounds. The app provides a peaceful space to relax, focus, and unwind.

No Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, track, or share any personal data or user information. You can use all features of the app without creating an account or providing any personal details.

Stress Free Flow does not use analytics, tracking tools, or third-party services that collect user data. Your experience is completely private and focused solely on your well-being.

All features of Stress Free Flow are self-contained within the app. No user data is transmitted to external servers at any time.

App Features & Functionality

The app includes a feature that allows users to share videos with a QR code. This code links directly to the App Store to share the app with others and does not facilitate external payments.

The Gentle Reminder function reminds users to return to the app for relaxation. This feature only runs when the app is closed and uses standard device functionality such as local notifications. No personal information is collected or transmitted.

Some features, such as ambient audio, may continue to play while the device is locked. These are user-initiated functions and do not involve any background data collection or tracking.

Background Audio

Stress Free Flow can be used as a background sound app (sound machine). Ambient audio can continue playing while the device is locked, which can support relaxation, sleep, or focused work.

This feature is optional and fully controlled by the user. To enable it, open Settings within the app, navigate to Audio Options / Vibration / Background Sound, and turn on “Play Sound When Locked / Background Sound.”

When enabled, the selected sound will continue playing while the device is locked. Users can control playback directly from the lock screen using standard iOS media controls, including pausing audio or switching between sounds.

Users can turn this feature off at any time within the app by disabling “Play Sound When Locked / Background Sound,” which will stop background playback.

Wellness Disclaimer

Stress Free Flow is a wellness app designed to provide calming visuals and ambient sounds for relaxation and focus. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
