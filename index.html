Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Stress Free Flow is a relaxation-focused application designed to help users reduce stress through calming visuals, interactive elements, and soothing ambient sounds.

This app does not collect, store, track, or share any personal data or user information. You can use all features of the app without creating an account or providing any personal details.

Stress Free Flow does not use analytics, tracking tools, or third-party services that collect user data. Your experience is completely private and focused solely on your well-being.

The app includes a feature that allows users to share videos with a QR code to share the app with friends. This code links directly to the App Store and does not facilitate external payments. Also the Gentle Reminder function reminds the user to use the relaxation app but will only execute if closed. Stress Free Flow is a wellness app designed to provide calming visuals and ambient sounds for relaxation and focus. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
